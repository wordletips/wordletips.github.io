Wordle FAQs

Q: How do I use the Wordle Assistant?
A: Enter your Wordle guess results in the main tool page. Use the green boxes for correct letters in correct positions, and follow the instructions there.

Q: What are some good starting words?
A: Try words with multiple vowels and common consonants like CRANE, ADIEU, or STARE.

Q: Can letters repeat in Wordle?
A: Yes, words can contain repeated letters (e.g., SHEEP, LLAMA).

Q: Are plural words included?
A: No, words ending in 'S' (like DESKS) are not included in Wordle answers.
